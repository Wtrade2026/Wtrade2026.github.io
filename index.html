How It Works

Simple, transparent process. Zero upfront cost to start.

1

You Tell Us What You Need

Send us product details, specifications, target price, and quantity. Photos, samples, or just a rough idea — we work with whatever you have.

2

We Find & Vet Suppliers

Within 3-7 days, we present 3-5 qualified factories with comparison: price, quality, MOQ, lead time, and our honest assessment.

3

You Choose, We Execute

Pick your preferred supplier. We negotiate final terms, arrange samples, and manage the order from deposit to delivery.

4

Quality Check & Ship

We inspect every order before it leaves the factory. Photos, videos, and a QC report sent to you. Only when you approve do we ship.
